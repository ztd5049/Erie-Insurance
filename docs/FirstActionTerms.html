Insurance Dictionary

First Action Terms

Accident Report Form

If you’re involved in an auto accident, the police officer on the scene produces this document, which is also known as the police report. This report has important details about the accident that will be useful to the insurance claims adjuster and it assists with processing and evaluating your claim.

At Fault

When someone’s involved in an accident, the insurance company has to figure out who caused it and how other drivers (if there were any) played a role. In the end, the insurance companies assign a percentage of fault to each driver. The insurance companies investigating the claims use these percentages to determine how much each insurance company pays to cover the costs of the damages.

Claim

If you're involved in a car accident, or something else happens that damages your car, one of the first things you'll do is report it to your insurance company — that's filing a claim. This begins the process so you can get reimbursed for your loss.

Real World Examples

Amy

Amy is a college student who just had her first car accident. While the damage was minimal, the police officer had her fill out an accident report form.

Jeff

A tree fell on Jeff's house and caused damage. Immediately, he filled out a claim so his insurance could help cover the damaged property.

Linda

Linda was in a car accident where her car was totaled. The other driver was found to be at fault because he ran a red light. After filing a claim, Linda was reinbursed when finding a new car.

Frequently Asked Questions

    How is it determined who is at fault?

  • At fault, or liability, will be determined based on the accident and resulting damage. It is possible that both victims are found responsible.

    • What happens after I file a claim?

  • After filing a claim, make sure to keep any affected items and document any of damage to the item. This is so your insurance adjuster can accurately estimate your damages.

    • Do you have to fill out an accident report form?

  • All drivers involved in an accident are required to fill out the accident report form to ensure the accident is well documented
